About A-nimal-Z

AnimalZ is a fun website created to help you learn about many interesting animals, from A to Z !
Ready to learn about your new favorite animal? Jump to any letter you want, let's go !

More about us

This website was created as a project to perhaps bring children some interesting new knowledge about the world of animals, while also having fun. You can explore animals from any letter of the alphabet, enabling a fun and interactive learning experience.

Moreover, this project aims to provide children with engaging knowledge about the animal kingdom. The ultimate goal is to ensure that learning about animals is both educational and enjoyable.
