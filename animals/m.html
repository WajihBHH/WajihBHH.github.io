About Monkeys

Monkeys are highly intelligent and social mammals belonging to the primate order, known for their dexterous hands and long tails.

They inhabit various habitats across the world, including forests, grasslands, and mountains, and are omnivorous, feeding on fruits, leaves, insects, and sometimes small animals.
