About Umbrellabirds

Umbrellabirds are large, black birds native to the rainforests of Central and South America, known for their distinctive crest resembling an umbrella.

They are primarily fruit-eaters but also consume insects and small vertebrates, and are often found in dense, lowland forests with a rich canopy cover.
